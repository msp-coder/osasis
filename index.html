About

We Help You Planning Your Journey

Oasis is an independent organization and headed by a Chief Executive and has veterans in the field of travel & tours. It is a dynamic destination management company promoting tourism around the world. It is into Group & FIT segments for leisure / business / Pilgrimage & incentive travel. Oasis specializes in Holy land & Christian Pilgrimage tours to Europe / Israel / Jordan & Egypt.

Oasis acts as a Wholesaler. It has a vast network of sub agents & retailers throughout India. Oasis is primarily dedicated to promoting holidaying options around the world. It also handles foreign shootings of Indian Cinema Industry at exotic foreign locations. In terms of volume of productions, Indian Cinema industry is as big as Hollywood.
